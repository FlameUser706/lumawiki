Whether you're looking to furnish your huge mansion or cozy cottage, our server's custom furniture
brings an extra layer of personalization to your builds!

You can find our furniture shop by talking to `Builder Bridgette` in `/spawn` or by using
`/furniture`. Each piece costs can vary from $1,000-$50,000 of in-game currency!

## Sets

Furniture sets can be unlocked through in-game or paid ranks or from **Furniture Vouchers**.
