Daily rewards can be claimed by players every 24 hours! You can claim your rewards by typing in
`/daily` or visiting Cowabunga in `/spawn`!

Rewards increase in value every day, so don't forget to keep checking in! Missing a day will result
in you being set back to day 1 rewards.
